Why is Python growing so fast? Python is used in a variety of purposes, ranging
from web development to data science to DevOps, and it’s worth understanding what
particular applications of Python have recently become more common. I’m a data
scientist who uses R, so I’m certainly interested in how much of Python’s growth
has been within my own field. In this post, I’ll take another look at Stack Overflow
data to understand what kinds of Python development have been growing, and in what
kinds of companies and organizations it’s most used.
